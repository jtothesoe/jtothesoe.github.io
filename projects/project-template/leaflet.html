Leaflet

Product Design

The design for leaflet was initially thought of as representation as a newly sprouting plant.

Leaflet was designed through hand sketches and sketch models, then digitally formed in Rhino. It was made using 3D print technology to accurately display all the curves and insets. The curves on each side is juxtaposed to supprt each otherthe curve for the top sprout and the curve for the base. The body is held with one hand that is ergnomically formed and the other hand in the bottom inset will further assist in lifting it up when heavy. This watering can was designed in mind to help the user from straining their back while lifting/pouring.
