Shure Chair

Product Design

Shure Chair mimics a piece of cloth that has been put over another framed chair. First started out with many different sketch models, but was intrigued mainly with the foam zippy sketch models.

On these sketch models, the curves were smooth and delicate- explaining each curve with one simple swoop. Although the curve seemed "simple", once it was digitized, it was an endless process of pulling and pushing points that could change the whole chair.
